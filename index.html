About Sky

Sky: Children of the Light is an adventure indie game developed and published by Thatgamecompany. It was released on July 18, 2019, for iOS, with subsequent releases on April 7, 2020, for Android and a planned port to Nintendo Switch during the summer of 2020.

Sky is all about navigating the realms, using your flight to reach new locations. When you first join the game, you are directed to the Isle of Dawn to begin earning your wings in the form of a cape. There are 80 Winged Light, scattered throughout the realms, which take the form of a glowing yellow Children of Light.
